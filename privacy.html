Privacy Policy for VisionDev

Last updated: July 30, 2025

1. Camera Access

VisionDev uses the camera permission solely to capture photos using motion detection. The app does not record video or take pictures without user initiation.

2. Storage Access

VisionDev requests access to your storage in order to save images locally or to your personal Google Drive account, if configured by the user. We do not access or upload any files without your consent.

3. Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data or media files. All processing remains local to your device or linked cloud storage.

4. Internet Permission

Internet access is used only to enable optional services such as uploading to Google Drive and displaying ads through AdMob. No user data is transmitted or stored externally.
